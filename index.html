Story

Why I Started This

The idea started in the early days of the pandemic. A few friends and I put together a small book club—mostly as an excuse to get out of the house.

What began as a casual gathering quickly became something more meaningful. We'd start by talking about the book, but the conversation rarely stayed there. It drifted into work, relationships, and whatever was going on in our lives.

A lot of nights ended the same way—sitting around a fire and talking long after the book discussion had ended. Over time, I realized how rare spaces like that can be. Most of us are busy and surrounded by people, yet don't have a place to simply show up and be ourselves.

The Evening Fire grew out of that idea—a small-group weekend retreat built around good food, time outside, shared experiences, and relaxed nights around the fire.

Your Host

About Me

I've spent the past decade working in tech and over time I noticed how easy it is to lose real connection with other guys, with myself, and with the natural world.

Most of my career has been built around people. I've worked in college recruitment, customer success, and as a youth instructor. Roles where showing up fully for others was the job. That same intention is what I bring into every Evening Fire retreat.

I'm also a certified yoga instructor, which shapes how I think about these weekends. There's a balance to it. Time together and time alone. Movement and rest. Comfort, without losing the simplicity of being outside.

I split my time between Vermont and the Pacific Northwest. I love being outdoors, spending time with my wife and dog, and building friendships.

Pricing & Booking

How much do retreats cost?

Most retreats fall between $900–$1,600 per person, depending on the location, length, and accommodations.

What's included in the price?

Everything for the weekend — meals, the stay, and the experience. You just show up.

How does booking work?

You'll apply to join a specific retreat. Once accepted, you'll receive full details and next steps to secure your spot.

What is the cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable, but can typically be applied to a future retreat if plans change.

Do you plan private retreats?

Yes—a limited number per year. Reach out by email to discuss shaping an experience around what you're looking for.

The Experience

What will we do during the retreat?

A mix of shared meals, outdoor activities, and guided conversations. There's structure for meaningful moments, but also plenty of space to relax.

How structured is it?

There's a schedule each day, but nothing is forced. You'll always know what's happening while having time to yourself.

How long are the retreats?

2–4 days, depending on the specific retreat.

What's the group size?

Intentionally small—typically 6–10 guys.

Do I need to know anyone coming?

No. Most guests come on their own.

Do people stay in touch after?

Some retreats are designed for men from the same city, so the connections you make don't end when the weekend does. A group chat gets started before the retreat and the rest is up to you.
